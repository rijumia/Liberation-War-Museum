Introduction

The Liberation War Archive stands as a testament to the sacrifices made by the people of Bangladesh in the fight for independence in 1971. It is an invaluable collection of documents, interviews, and photographs that chronicle the events, struggles, and triumphs of the Liberation War. The archive preserves the history and heritage of the nation, serving as a vital resource for research, education, and remembrance.
