Interview with Freedom Fighter - Sheikh Rahman

Sheikh Rahman played a crucial role during the Liberation War and fought in the frontlines. His testimony covers his experiences and the challenges he faced.

View Details

Biography: Sheikh Rahman was a prominent freedom fighter who led a guerrilla warfare unit during the war. He was instrumental in organizing underground resistance efforts.

Interview Summary: In this interview, Sheikh Rahman talks about the challenges, his personal sacrifices, and the strategies used in the battle for independence.

Eyewitness Account by Amina Begum

Amina Begum witnessed the horrors of the war firsthand. In her interview, she shares the experiences of civilian life during the liberation struggle.

View Details

Biography: Amina Begum was a school teacher who witnessed the atrocities committed during the war, and she recalls her experiences of the liberation movement.

Interview Summary: Amina shares her account of what it was like for civilians, describing the violence, loss, and hope amidst the war.

About Us

The Liberation War Archive is a collection of documents, interviews, and photographs that preserves the history of the Bangladesh Liberation War of 1971. It serves as a resource for research, education, and remembrance.
